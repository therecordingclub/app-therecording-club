Our Spaces

Six Distinct Rooms. One Venue.

Every space is purpose-built for creativity, production, and hospitality.

01

The Live Room

The centerpiece of every event.

Our flagship performance and event space, built for live music, branded activations, private concerts, and broadcast production. The room features a 15-foot LED wall for immersive visuals, concert-grade sound, and professional lighting — all production-ready from the moment you walk in.

Mixing Allen & Heath SQ7
Lighting GrandMA3 Console
Video Blackmagic ATEM TV Studio HD 8 ISO
Cameras 8× Blackmagic Studio 6K Pro
Display 15' LED Wall
02

The Mixing Room

Where every performance is captured.

Adjacent to the Live Room, our Mixing Room records every note of your event in studio quality. With a 40-channel analog console feeding directly into Pro Tools, your live event becomes a releasable recording — or a broadcast-ready mix in real time.

Console 40-Channel Trident 88
DAW Pro Tools
Monitors Ocean Way Audio Speakers & Subs
03

The Recording Room

Studio-grade tracking and production.

A fully equipped recording studio featuring a baby grand piano, Vintage Vibe keys, drums, bass and guitar amplification, and an isolated vocal booth. Perfect for pre-event rehearsal recordings, content creation during your event, or standalone sessions.

Keys Baby Grand Piano & Vintage Vibe
Amplification Bass Amp & Guitar Amp
Tracking Drums & Vocal Booth
04

The Podcast Studio

Broadcast-ready from day one.

A purpose-built podcast and interview studio outfitted with three Blackmagic Studio 6K Pro cameras and three dedicated microphones. Produce professional multi-camera video podcasts, interviews, panel discussions, and branded content — all captured simultaneously in cinema-quality resolution.

Cameras 3× Blackmagic Studio 6K Pro
Audio 3× Dedicated Microphones
Output Multi-Camera Video & Audio
05

The Rehearsal Room

Prepare, practice, perfect.

A fully outfitted rehearsal space with a large recording booth, drums, bass and guitar amps, keys, and PMC reference monitors. Ideal for artists preparing for live performances, pre-production sessions, or warm-ups before taking the main stage in the Live Room.

Backline Drums, Bass Amp, Guitar Amp, Keys
Monitors PMC Speakers
Recording Large Isolation Booth
06

Lobby & Hospitality

Where first impressions are made.

An elegant lobby with a full bar space sets the tone the moment guests arrive. Our standard home kitchen and dining area accommodate up to 40 seated guests — perfect for intimate dinners, VIP receptions, cocktail hours, and corporate hospitality alongside your main event.

Dining Seating for 40 Guests
Bar Full Bar in Elegant Lobby
Kitchen Standard Home Kitchen

Production Capabilities

Everything You Need. Nothing You Don't.

Four integrated production disciplines working together seamlessly.

Audio

Concert-grade FOH mixing, 40-channel analog recording, reference monitoring, and dedicated isolation booths across multiple rooms.

Video

Up to 8 Blackmagic Studio 6K Pro cameras, ATEM Television Studio HD 8 ISO switching, multi-camera production, and cinema-quality capture.

Lighting

GrandMA3 console controlling a full programmable lighting rig paired with our 15-foot LED wall for immersive visual environments.

Hospitality

Full kitchen and bar service, dining for 40 guests, elegant lobby reception, and dedicated hospitality staff for seamless event management.
